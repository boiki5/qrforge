FAQs

ü§î General Questions

Q: What is a static QR code?
A: A static QR code permanently encodes your URL into the pattern. Once generated, it cannot be changed or updated.

Q: How is this different from dynamic QR codes?
A: Dynamic codes use short URLs that can be edited later. Static codes embed the full URL directly - permanent but uneditable.

Q: Why should I use QRForge?
A: Complete privacy (no data stored), permanent codes, free access, and beautiful, customizable output.

üîê Privacy & Security

Q: Why do I need to "login" with affiliate links?

Q: Is my data safe?

Q: Why won't my QR code scan properly?
